Game Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the round.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your selected crystal point counter.
  • You win the round by matching your selected crystal counter value to the random number.
  • You lose the round if your selected crystal counter value goes above the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time a new round starts, the values of the crystals will change.
